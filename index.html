About zkGhost?

Built on the zkSync network, zkGhost is a community-owned fair launch token. The project initiative seeks to familiarize individuals

with the zkSync ecosystem using ghosts that represent people feeling lonely in their lives.

Ensure that a peg of Ghost: ETH is achieved.

It is maintained to establish $zkGhost as a mirrored, liquid asset to $ETH.

The goal of zkGhost would be to provide a community for individuals who feel isolated or disconnected, where they can connect

with others who share similar interests and experiences. The token would be used to access exclusive content, participate in

community events, and receive discounts on products and services related to the theme of the community.
